What is this?

This is my only private coaching container available.

I work with coaches, athletes, creators, and entrepreneurs who are having some success — but something is still off. They aren’t breaking through to the new heights they want to, and they don’t feel entirely fulfilled in their work or performance.

My coaching draws from ecological dynamics, skill acquisition science, and perception-action theory — applied directly to your domain and your specific constraints.

The work is diagnostic, not prescriptive.

I believe there are no cookie-cutter solutions to performance plateaus. Each problem is unique, and each requires a precise environmental redesign to transcend.

I function as a performance coach and diagnostic mirror in your pocket — noticing blindspots, challenging assumptions, and helping you redesign the conditions producing your current results.

How it works

We begin with a 75-minute Environmental Audit to clarify your goals, map your current constraints, and diagnose exactly what is producing your plateau.

From there, we check in via voice note or text every day. It is expected that you send a fully transparent 3–5 minute check-in each day.

This serves as an ongoing diagnostic loop and allows us to iterate and realign faster than any once-a-week program. The constraint gets adjusted in real time — not a week later when the moment has passed.

Throughout our time together, I will also send you relevant frameworks and constraint designs specific to your domain to accelerate your progress.

You will receive access to the full Foundations course and workbook for the duration of your engagement.
